FAQ

Questions We Hear
Most Often.

Can't find your answer? Email us at contact@thegenzhr.com

What types of businesses do you work with? +
We primarily serve SMEs, early-stage startups, and NGOs across Nigeria and Africa. We also work with larger organisations that have existing HR teams but need specialist support for specific projects. For brands, recruiters, and employers, we offer media and sponsorship access to our 106,000-person professional community.
How does the remote HR service actually work? +
All HR services are delivered fully remotely using the latest tools — WhatsApp, Zoom, Slack, email, and HR management platforms. You get a dedicated consultant and account manager, weekly or on-demand advisory, and all deliverables (policies, reports, payroll) handled digitally. Many clients tell us they receive more active HR support with us than they ever had from an in-house team.
How is pricing structured for the HR packages? +
All packages are monthly subscriptions. Pricing is tailored based on your team size, specific scope of services, and business stage. We don't publish fixed prices because we believe in right-sizing the engagement to your actual needs. Contact us and we'll turn around a tailored proposal within 48 hours.
How do media and sponsorship packages work? +
Brands, recruiters, and companies pay to reach our 106,000-person community through newsletter placements, WhatsApp broadcasts, social media campaigns, company spotlights, and event sponsorships. You send us your brief and desired CTA. Our editorial team writes the copy. You approve it before anything goes live. After the campaign, you receive a performance report. Email partnerships@thegenzhr.com to request our media kit.
What's included in your recruitment guarantee? +
For Full-Cycle Recruitment (10% of annual gross salary), we provide a 60-day replacement guarantee — if the hire doesn't work out within 60 days, we replace them at no additional cost. For the Sourcing + Shortlist Service (₦200,000 flat fee), we offer a 30-day replacement guarantee. Both guarantees apply as long as role requirements haven't materially changed.
Who is career consulting suitable for? +
Our career consulting serves three groups: young professionals and Gen Z who need direction, clarity, and career launch support; mid-career professionals navigating pivots, transitions, or plateaus; and senior professionals working on promotion strategy and leadership positioning. Book a discovery call and we'll tell you honestly whether we're the right fit.
Can I start with a lower HR package and upgrade? +
Absolutely. Many clients start on Bronze and upgrade as their team grows. All packages are designed to layer — every higher tier includes everything in the tiers below it. Upgrades can typically be activated within one billing cycle with no disruption to ongoing work.
